Me

Bridging the gap between design token and code variables

Hey, I’m Deni — a product owner turned aspiring software developer based in Jakarta. After years shaping tech products in the fintech and agritech space, from securities crowdfunding at SHAFIQ to supply chain platforms like Empofarm, I’m now channeling that strategic mindset into building things with code. I love designing systems that make life simpler and more secure for users, whether it’s crafting intuitive dashboards or architecting seamless financial flows. When I’m not translating business needs into product specs, you’ll probably find me tinkering with React, leveling up my UI skills, or exploring how tech can empower the underbanked. I’m driven by clarity, guided by curiosity, and always up for a challenge that blends design, logic, and impact. Educational Background
